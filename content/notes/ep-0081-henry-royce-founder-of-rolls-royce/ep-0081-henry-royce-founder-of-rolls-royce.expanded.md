### 1:06:00 — Royce’s monastic, phone‑free design office

Context: After a near‑fatal illness forced Royce to work remotely, a small design team operated in seclusion near his house. The environment was deliberately stripped of distractions to protect his creative output.

Quote: "They worked in **monastic seclusion**, like monks, in a drawing office called Camacha, situated in the middle of a village about 0.25 mile from Royce's house. Key takeaway: Radical environment design can defend deep work and sustain a founder’s most valuable asset—uninterrupted thought.
