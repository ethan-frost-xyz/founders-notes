Context: John Bogle critiques the business world’s excessive reliance on quantifiable metrics, arguing that it overshadows the importance of trust and human judgment. Key takeaway: Bogle emphasizes that an obsession with counting and data can crowd out the trust-based relationships essential for long-term success.
