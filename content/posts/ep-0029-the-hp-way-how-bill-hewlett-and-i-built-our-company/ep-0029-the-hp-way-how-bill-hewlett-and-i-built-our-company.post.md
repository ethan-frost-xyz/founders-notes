Founders #29: David Packard (Hewlett-Packard $HPQ)

Packard led HP in various executive roles for about 46 years. As a rancher, he naturally thought of managing over 50,000 people like herding cattle: 

“Every season, we would round up the cattle… I found after much trial and error that applying steady, gentle pressure worked best. Eventually, one would decide to pass through the gate, and the rest would soon follow. Press them too hard, and they panic… Slack off entirely and they just head back to their old grazing spots.” (18:30)
