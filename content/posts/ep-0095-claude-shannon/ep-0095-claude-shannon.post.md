Founders: #95 Claude Shannon

Shannon emphasized learning multiple disciplines aligned with your interests, contrasting many founders who favor specialization in the world of infinite leverage. Ironically, Shannon paved the way for the Information Age, and coined the term "bit". https://t.co/WL9Ld5SZRZ

Align your interests with what you do. Follow that natural "drift". (31:30)

"I’ve been working on 3 different ideas simultaneously. And strangely enough, it seems a more productive method than sticking to one problem." (33:00)

Success is sustainable when you trust your instincts and ignore prestige over personal curiosity. (42:30)

"I think the history of science has shown that valuable consequences often proliferate from simple curiosity." (44:00, 54:30)
