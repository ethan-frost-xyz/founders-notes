Founders #9 Henry Ford

Ford had one idea for 20 years and refused to let it go. Everyone should be able to afford a car.

You don’t actually need fifty ideas. You need one clear vision, driven by a level of violence in execution (s/o Gen. Patton), through mass-production supremacy, with cost-cutting serving as a driver of both profits and marketing.
