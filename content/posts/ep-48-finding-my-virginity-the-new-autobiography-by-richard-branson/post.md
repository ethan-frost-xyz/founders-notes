Founders #48 Richard Branson - Virgin

A somewhat dull episode, however Senra offers a simple yet profound observation: “Being a good parent is the best thing that you can do to have a positive impact in the world.” If this doesn’t resonate, it might be worth looking inwards…

Senra continues, “Kids can grow up in just really wretched, putrid environments, and those kids are influenced by their environment, they become the adults and they do the same thing. You’ve got to break the cycle somewhere.” As someone trying to “break the cycle,” this small insight is what makes listening to these episodes all worth it. (36:00)
