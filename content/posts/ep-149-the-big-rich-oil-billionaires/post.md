Founders #149: Roy Cullen, Clint Murchison & H.L. Hunt

These Texas wildcatters turned years of dry holes, massive debt, and expert skepticism into some of the greatest independent fortunes in history by simply refusing to quit and betting big on their own convictions.

Here are their best ideas:

Never quit on your family, no matter how hard it gets.
Cullen pushed through three straight years of brutal exhaustion and repeated failures at Damon Mound. His daily promise to his wife that tomorrow would be another day kept him going for their children’s future. (18:00)

Go deeper where everyone else stops. 
Cullen’s entire edge came from drilling further into wells that competitors had already abandoned. This simple “a little deeper” rule became his lifelong strategy and literally defined him until the grave. (20:00)

Your industry can be bigger than entire nations. 
During WWII, Texas alone produced nearly double the oil output of all Axis powers combined. These independent founders operated on a scale that literally fueled world events. (59:00)
