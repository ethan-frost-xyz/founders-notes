#78: Charlie Munger

Founders #78: Charlie Munger (#1 - Tao of Charlie Munger)

"Principles are truths you apply to your life." —Ray Dalio

If you agree, Munger is the gold standard.

With over 7 Founders episodes about him, I will focus on his best principles one at a time.

#1: Circle of Competence

“Knowing what you don't know is more useful than being brilliant." - Munger

Know exactly what you know and what you don’t. Operating inside your circle is safe, operating outside it is like gambling.

Furthermore, the size of the circle matters less than the rigidity of its boundaries.

Expanding into Munger’s broader philosophies that pair well with Circle of Competence…

You don’t need to be an expert on 99% of things. You just need to be right about the 1% you actually bet on.
