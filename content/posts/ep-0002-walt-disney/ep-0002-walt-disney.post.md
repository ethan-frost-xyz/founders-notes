Founders #2 Walt Disney.

What would Disney think of Sora? In some ways, he played a significant part in the story. What he created and the people he inspired with animation and in cinema ended up becoming a meaningful chunk of Sora's data.

Before his fame, Disney worked seven days a week for three years developing Snow White, with everyone, including his wife, doubting him and telling him that nobody would ever watch a 2-hour cartoon. It broke every box-office record and changed cinema forever.

No doubt a young Disney would adopt these tools today.
