Founders #119 Dodge Brothers

Two small-town machinists who supplied the guts of Henry Ford’s empire.

They made the parts everyone else needed during the auto gold rush.

While others chased the dream of building cars, the Dodge brothers made the transmissions, axles, and parts that every early automaker desperately needed.

“In a gold rush, don’t dig for gold. Sell pickaxes.” (24:30)
