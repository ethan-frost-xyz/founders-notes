Founders #42: Dee Hock (Visa $V)

Hock did his best to prevent the monopoly his creation established, which still exists today (w/ MasterCard). He wanted free markets for payment processing (1:05:00) however, I believe Hock would hate most alt coins...

Using Hock’s “words” supports this claim. Tokenomics as "Compelling" behavior (exploitation of retail investors using alt coins) instead of "Educing" behavior (people using it because of shared purpose or utility). He would warn that any network sustained only by financial incentives (“Avarice’), rather than shared principles, will inevitably collapse.

“nothin finer” (17:30)

Companies die when they lose excitement about the future (56:30)

Note: in 2015 Hock was quoted saying he liked bitcoin and was an advisor to a wallet company.
