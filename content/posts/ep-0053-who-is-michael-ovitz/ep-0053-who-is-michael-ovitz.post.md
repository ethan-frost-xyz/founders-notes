Founders #53 Michael Ovitz (CAA, $DIS)

There is a lot to love and hate about Ovitz. Through the first 50 episodes, he is simultaneously one of the most impressive yet Machiavellian subjects. Ovitz betrayed many people close to him for minimal benefit on his way to becoming one of, if not the most powerful men in Hollywood, before losing it all. Key lesson: “Don’t mix business and relationships; business always gets personal.” (1:29:30). 

Also Ovitz had the CAA office designed by I.M. Pei right after he finished the Louvre. Insane.
