Founders #21: John Carmack and John Romero (Doom)

This episode is really two founder origin stories running in parallel until they collide. Carmack is a cracked technical founder who had this open-source “hackers” philosophy, which led to their revolutionary video game distribution strategies that paved the way for the freemium model. Romero was the charismatic spokesman who had strong business instincts, who also overcame a brutal upbringing, which is highlighted in detail.

Senra uses them to demonstrate how far you can go with this classic pairing, but also how easily things can unravel. Overall, this episode is more about storytelling than quick punchlines (which isn't a bad thing), and I think it's better than #20.
