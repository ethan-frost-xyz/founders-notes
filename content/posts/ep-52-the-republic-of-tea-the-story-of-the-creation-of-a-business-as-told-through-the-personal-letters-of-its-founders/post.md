Founders #52: Mel Ziegler & Bill Rosenzweig (Republic of Tea)

If you find it hard to move from plan to action, this is a great listen. The episode covers the story of idea generation, or the “prequel” to the lucrative exit two years after launching. Senra has great points, but he missed one key insight:

Mel (Banana Republic founder) goes on a silent Buddhist retreat specifically to cut off communication with Bill (first time founder). This was a strategic mentorship move to force Bill to stop “building plans” and face the "terror" of his own decision making. My take: sometimes the best way to mentor is by removing support.
