Founders #20: Danny Meyer (Shake Shack, USHG)

Honestly, not my favorite episode. I think when this was recorded, Shake Shake was at its peak of popularity. That's not to say it should discredit what Senra is highlighting or what Meyers did. I found the episode to lack a punch. Nice quote about handling adversity: “The road to success is paved with mistakes well handled” (41:37). Afterwards Senra highlights Meyer’s belief that pursuing perfection is costly. I understand the point, but it's more interesting that it contradicts exactly what I highlighted in #19 from Steve Jobs’ time at Pixar.
