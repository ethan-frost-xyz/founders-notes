#19 Becoming Steve Jobs

A key lesson Jobs learned at Pixar was that the work should not be driven by the deadline and that you must keep at the difficult parts until they are perfected.

“At Pixar, on almost every film they make, something turns out to be not quite right. And they have an amazing willingness to turn around and do it again, till they do get it right. They have always had a willingness to not be governed by the release date. It's not about how fast you do something, it's about doing your level best." - Steve Jobs (52:19)

Will Rockstar deliver?

Given the track record, I believe they will. It takes a ton of courage to delay this multiple times as a public company.
