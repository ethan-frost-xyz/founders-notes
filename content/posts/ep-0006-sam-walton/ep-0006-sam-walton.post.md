Founders #6 Sam Walton. An interesting wrinkle in Walmart’s success is the timely arrival of commercial private aviation: 1) scouting the expansion in rural markets, a key component of Walmart's thesis; and 2) enabling Walton to monitor every location on the ground, a unique attribute of Walton that he would use throughout his leadership.
