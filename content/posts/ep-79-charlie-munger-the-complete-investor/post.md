Founders #79: Charlie Munger (#2 - The Complete Investor)

"To the man with a hammer, every problem tends to look pretty much like a nail” - Charlie Munger

Charlie believes relying on a single mental model is a huge mistake.

That’s why Charlie uses what he describes as a Latticework of Mental Models.

In short, you need to fill your thought processes with principles and mental models from different disciplines because real-world problems don’t respect academic boundaries.

Charlie notes in his book that you need “only” 80-90 are needed 90% of the time.

Practically speaking, he later mentioned that seven models can handle most of the work.
