Founders #47 Richard Branson (pt. 1) - Virgin

This is more of a story episode, not filled with many actionable insights, but I found it interesting (everything about Branson is interesting): Branson expanded Virgin not as a traditional conglomerate but as what he called a "branded venture capital," leveraging his brand and cash for 50/50 partnerships with various ventures, ultimately reaching over 400 companies at the peak.
