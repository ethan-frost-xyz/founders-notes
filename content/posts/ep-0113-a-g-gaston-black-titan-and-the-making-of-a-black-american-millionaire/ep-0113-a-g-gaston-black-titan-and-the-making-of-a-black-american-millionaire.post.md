Founders #113

A.G. Gaston grew up in a log cabin in Alabama with grandparents who had been slaves.

After serving in WWI, Gaston, with only a 10th-grade education, built an estate worth over $200M.

The question he always asked was, “A young person should keep his eyes open. They should study the people around them. How do they live? What makes them tick, what do they need?” (24:45)
