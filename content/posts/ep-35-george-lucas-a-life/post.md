Founders #35: George Lucas (Star Wars)

Biggest takeaway is that Lucas was obsessed with control. 

“It's really become binary. Either you do it yourself or you don't get a say.”

“I was not that willing to listen to other people's ideas. I wanted everything to be my way. I didn't mind getting input from the creative people around me, but not the executives.”

“What we're striving for is total freedom, where we can finance our pictures, make them our way, release them when we want them released and be completely free to express ourselves... In this country, the only thing that speaks is money, and you have to have the money in order to have the power to be free.”
